Specialist tax consulting and technical support for businesses, law firms and accounting practices.

Taxation law is complex. We take complex solutions and make them simple by ensuring we have a thorough understanding of your goals and providing tailored taxation advice in language that makes sense to you.

How we can support your business

Our tax lawyers and consultants offer unique in-house commercial and Australian Taxation Office (ATO) experience. We know what it is like to be both the client and the regulator. This enables us to interpret tax law through different lenses and helps us to achieve the best and most commercial outcomes through specialist advice relating to tax minimisation, the tax-efficient repatriation of funds and tax-efficient business sales, both globally and domestically. We can also help you to overcome complex taxation challenges by providing specialist advice on isolated domestic and international tax law issues.

OUR TAX LAW AND CONSULTANCY SERVICES

Together with our Corporate Legal Team, our Tax Consultants ensure that any structure we recommend protects your assets and that the implementation process is seamless.

  • International and domestic structuring
  • International transactions advice and repatriation strategies
  • Complex domestic tax advice
  • Tax review of legal documentation
  • Mergers and acquisitions
  • Stamp duties advice
  • GST advice

Helping businesses of all shapes and sizes

Start-Ups

We help founders to establish the most effective business structure from the outset, ensuring tax minimisation, attractiveness to investors and eligibility to claim the R&D Tax Incentive where required.

Scale-Ups

Our team works with scale-up businesses to optimise their business structure as their business evolves, considering the tax and risk implications balanced with asset protection. We provide detailed advice in relation to the operation of the optimal business structure, in-depth tax reviews of transactions, ongoing tax advice in relation to isolated issues and calculated repatriation strategies to assist with the successful growth of scale-up businesses operations.

Exit

We support business owners by delivering asset maximisation strategies, transaction structuring, repatriation strategies and ensuring the most tax-efficient exit when they decide to sell. From time to time, this includes any potential restructuring required to make this happen.

Our tax specialists

As specialists in both Australian and international tax law, our Tax Consultants and Lawyers help you to achieve tax outcomes that ultimately serve your vision of business success.

Led by Associate Director of ABA Legal Group, Toni Eisenhut, our Tax Team have many years of combined experience and understand both the common and unique challenges faced by businesses just like yours. As leader of the Tax Team, Toni brings with her over 12 years experience specialising in Corporate and International Tax with a particular focus on international expansion, inbound and outbound investment, structuring and repatriation strategies.

Working closely with you, Toni and the team are here to support your business through the challenges and opportunities presented to you.
